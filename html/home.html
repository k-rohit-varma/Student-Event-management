Student Extra Curricular activity Management System

About the System

The Student Extra Curricular activity Management System is designed to facilitate the organization, management, and participation in various student events. It serves as a platform where students can view upcoming events, register for participation, and receive notifications about event updates.

How It Helps

  • Streamlines event registration and management.
  • Provides a centralized platform for all event-related information.
  • Enhances student engagement through easy access to events.
  • Enables real-time updates and notifications.
  • Facilitates collaboration among student organizations.

How It Works

Users can register on the platform and create profiles. Once logged in, students can browse events, sign up for activities, and manage their registrations. Organizers can create and manage events, track registrations, and communicate with participants through the system.
